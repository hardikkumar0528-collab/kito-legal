Delete your KITO account

Last updated: 18 July 2026

This page explains how to delete your KITO account and what happens to your data. You do not need to have the app installed to make a request.

App name: KITO · Package: com.studyquest.app

Option 1 — Delete it yourself in the app

  1. Open KITO.
  2. Go to the Profile tab.
  3. Scroll to the bottom and tap Delete my account.
  4. Confirm by tapping Delete forever.

Your account and study data are deleted straight away. This cannot be undone.

Option 2 — Ask us to delete it by email

If you cannot install the app, have lost access to your account, or are a parent or guardian requesting deletion on behalf of a child, email:

kito.team.support@gmail.com

Please include the email address on the KITO account and the subject line "Delete my account". If you are a parent or guardian, say so — we will action the request without needing the student to be involved.

We respond within 30 days, and usually much sooner. We may ask you to confirm from the account's own email address, so that someone else cannot delete your data.

What gets deleted

  • Your account and sign-in details (name, email address)
  • Your study plans, snapped notes, saved doubts and practice tests
  • Your streak, coins, study minutes and all other progress
  • Everything stored on your device by the app

This is permanent. Deleted data cannot be recovered.

What we keep, and for how long

  • Records we are legally required to keep — for example, if you made a purchase, basic transaction records may be retained for as long as tax and accounting law requires. These are not linked to your study data.
  • Backups. Your data may persist in encrypted backups for up to 30 days before those backups expire, after which it is gone.

We do not keep a shadow copy of your study data, and we do not sell personal information to anyone.

Photos you sent to KITO's AI features

Photos you snapped (of a syllabus, chapter page or question) were sent to our AI provider only to generate that material for you. They are not used to train AI models and are not retained by us. Deleting your account removes the notes, plans and tests that were generated from them.

Cancelling a paid subscription

Deleting your KITO account does not automatically cancel a Google Play subscription. Cancel it separately:

  1. Open the Google Play Store app.
  2. Tap your profile icon → Payments & subscriptions → Subscriptions.
  3. Select KITO and tap Cancel subscription.
Cancel your subscription before deleting your account, otherwise you may keep being billed for a subscription attached to an account that no longer exists.
